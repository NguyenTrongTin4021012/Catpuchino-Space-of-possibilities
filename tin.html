Turbulence

- by Nguyen Trong Tin

Rote learning restricts one's ability to learn, to partake and explore. It hinders the mind, given no room to grow.

This artwork was supposed to express how negatively it left me feeling: unguided, unaccommodated, chaotic.

This made me think of turbulence, the wild powerful force behind raging storms and typhoons.
