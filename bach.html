Disrupted Saturn

Disturbed Saturn is an generative art inspired by the structures of Saturn to convey how challenging and ineffective that rote learning method.

Like the stormy environment of Saturn, the education systems and some of their ways of enforcing students has not been affective, it is fast and rapid without helping the students understand the knowledge that was giving to.

This effect to the future of student as they only memorizing only what they have learn but lack skills to analyze and learn new knowledge, and any difficulties or challenge might make them shy away and “fade away” from the system.
